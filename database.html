Marijuana

About
Marijuana is the name of the dried leaves, flowers, stems, and seeds from the Cannabis sativa or Cannabis indica plant. The main active chemical is tetrahydrocannabinol often referred to as THC. Marijuana is the most commonly used illegal drug in the US.
Effects
Effects include altered senses, altered sense of time, changes in mood, impaired body movement, difficulty with thinking and problem-solving, impaired memory, hallucinations , breathing problems, etc.
